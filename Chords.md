# Chord theory

## Chord structure

- Major:      `1 3 5`
- Major 7th:  `1 3 5 7`
- Major 9th:  `1 3 5 7 9`
- Major 11th: `1 3 5 7 9 11`

- Minor:      `1 b3 5`
- Minor 7th:  `1 b3 5 7`
- Minor 9th:  `1 b3 5 7 9`
- Minor 11th: `1 b3 5 7 9 11`

- 7:    `1 3 5 b7`
- 9:    `1 3 5 b7 9`
- 11:   `1 3 5 b7 9 11`
- 13:   `1 3 5 b7 9 11 13`

- sus2: `1 2 5` ?
- sus4: `1 4 5`

- augmented:  `1 3 #5`
- diminished: `1 b3 b5`
- dim 7:      `1 b3 bb7(6)`
- half dim:   `1 b3 b5 b7`

- 6: `1 3 5 6`
- minor 6: `1 b5 5 6`
- min/maj 7: `1 b3 5 7`
- 6/9: `1 3 5 6 9`
